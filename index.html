Who Are We

The annual festival is produced each year by its executive committee with the support of the city of Trenton, Mercer County, and sponsors like you.

Tell me about the festival

The Trenton African American Cultural Festival is an annual celebration of the history, culture, heritage, and arts that represent the rich traditions and zestful spirit of African contributions throughout the diaspora. Following the legacy of the African American culture's Juneteenth celebration, the goal of the festival is to inspire an appreciation for the diversity, influence and contributions of the African Diaspora.
